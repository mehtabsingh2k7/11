Mediacal Careers

Explore Various Mediacal Fields, Courses, and Job Opportunities

Clinical Medicine

  • What it is: Treating patients, diagnosing illnesses.
  • Qualification: MBBS after 12th (Science with Biology).
  • Career Benefits: Doctor, specialist; high respect and salary.
  • Work Areas: Hospitals, clinics, private practice.

Biomedical Science

  • What it is: Research about diseases and treatments.
  • Qualification: B.Sc. in Biomedical Science.
  • Career Benefits: Researcher, pharma companies, work abroad opportunities.
  • Work Areas: Labs, hospitals, research centers.

Pharmacology

  • What it is: Study of medicines and their effects.
  • Qualification: B.Pharm after 12th.
  • Career Benefits: Pharmacist, drug researcher, pharma industry jobs.
  • Work Areas: Pharmacies, hospitals, pharma companies.

Public Health

  • What it is: Keeping communities healthy and disease-free.
  • Qualification: BPH or MBBS + MPH.
  • Career Benefits: Government jobs, NGOs, international organizations.
  • Work Areas: Health departments, NGOs, WHO.

Forensic Science

  • What it is: Solving crimes using scientific methods.
  • Qualification: B.Sc. in Forensic Science.
  • Career Benefits: Work with police, CBI, detective agencies.
  • Work Areas: Crime labs, police departments, legal firms.

Pathology

  • What it is: Studying body tissues and fluids to diagnose diseases.
  • Qualification: MBBS + specialization in Pathology or B.Sc. MLT.
  • Career Benefits: Work in labs, hospitals; good income, high demand.
  • Work Areas: Hospitals, labs, research centers.

Anatomy and Physiology

  • What it is: Study of body structure and function.
  • Qualification: B.Sc. in Anatomy, Physiology or Life Sciences.
  • Career Benefits: Teaching, research, healthcare careers.
  • Work Areas: Colleges, labs, hospitals.
