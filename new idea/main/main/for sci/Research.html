Research & Development (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)

What is R&D?

Research and Development means discovering new ideas and creating better products, medicines, or technologies. Whether it's studying atoms, chemicals, or living organisms â€” R&D is about making the future better!

Career Options

  • Physics: Work on space research, lasers, energy solutions.
  • Chemistry: Create new medicines, plastics, cosmetics, and fuels.
  • Biology: Discover vaccines, improve farming, or fight diseases.

Qualifications Needed

  • Pass 12th grade with Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Maths).
  • Study B.Sc. in Physics, Chemistry, or Biology.
  • Then pursue M.Sc. for deeper knowledge.
  • For top research jobs, a Ph.D. is highly recommended!

Job Benefits

  • Work in top companies like ISRO, DRDO, Biocon, and Tata.
  • Opportunities to work in government research labs and universities.
  • Chance to invent something that can change the world!
  • High respect, good salary, and even chances to work abroad.

Where Can You Work?

  • Research labs (like CSIR, IISc, BARC).
  • Pharmaceutical companies and biotech firms.
  • Government organizations (DRDO, ISRO, ICMR).
  • Colleges and Universities as a researcher or professor.
