Your Guide to Careers in Science

Introduction to Science Stream

The Science Stream is one of the most popular streams chosen by students after 12th grade. It opens doors to various career paths in fields such as Engineering, Medicine, Research, and more. Whether you're interested in pursuing a career in healthcare, technology, or space exploration, the Science stream offers numerous opportunities for you.

Career Options After Science

Through innovation and problem solving, engineers design solutions that improve everyday life. With strong demand across industries, engineering provides excellent career growth, high salaries, and opportunities to build a better future.
